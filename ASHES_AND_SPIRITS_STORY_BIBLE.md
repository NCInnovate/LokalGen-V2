## 📖 SERIES OVERVIEW

**Title:** Ashes & Spirits  
**Setting:** Rabaul, East New Britain Province, Papua New Guinea  
**Time Period:** July 1994 - December 1998 (5-year recovery arc)  
**Total Episodes:** 200 (10 seasons × 20 episodes)  
**Genre:** Historical Fiction with Supernatural Elements (Based on True Events)  
**Tone:** Epic, emotional, spiritual, hopeful  

### Series Premise

When the earth beneath Rabaul begins to tremble in July 1994, whispers spread through villages of spiritual warnings—messages from ancestors, dreams of fire, and the voices of the land itself crying out. But few listen until it's too late. On September 19, 1994, Mount Tavurvur and Vulcan erupt simultaneously, destroying a city and changing thousands of lives forever.

This is the story of survival, love, community, and the mysterious spiritual forces that guide humanity through catastrophe. Follow interconnected characters across 10 seasons as they navigate the physical and spiritual aftermath of nature's fury, discovering that sometimes the greatest transformations come from total destruction.

*While all characters and individual stories are fictional, they are grounded in the real historical eruption and inspired by the real experiences of those who lived through it.*

## 🌍 WORLD-BUILDING

### Pre-Eruption Rabaul (July-September 1994)

**Geography:**
- Coastal city on Gazelle Peninsula
- Surrounded by volcanoes (Tavurvur, Vulcan, Matupit)
- Deep harbor, fishing community
- Mix of colonial architecture and traditional villages
- Population: ~23,000

**Culture:**
- Blend of indigenous Tolai culture and colonial British influence
- Strong family/clan structures
- Deep spiritual traditions (ancestor veneration)
- Fishing, trading, small-scale agriculture
- Community-centered decision-making

**Economic Hub:**
- Copra (dried coconut) export
- Fishing industry
- Government administrative center
- Tourism & hospitality

### The Spirits of Ashes & Spirits

**Spirit Classification:**

1. **Ancestor Spirits (Kapuna)**
   - Deceased family/community members
   - Guide and protect bloodline descendants
   - Can communicate through dreams, signs, symbols
   - Strongest during crisis

2. **Land Spirits (Tutuana)**
   - Connected to earth, volcanoes, ocean
   - Pre-human entities
   - Protective but unpredictable
   - Communicate through natural phenomena

3. **Messenger Spirits (Palauna)**
   - Neutral intermediaries
   - Deliver warnings and prophecies
   - Can manifest as animals, light, voice
   - Appear before major events

4. **Shadow Spirits (Batuata)**
   - Not evil, but challenging
   - Test human character
   - Force growth through adversity
   - Eventually assist if respected

5. **Healing Spirits (Momuna)**
   - Appear after destruction
   - Facilitate recovery and transformation
   - Work through human compassion
   - Strongest in seasons 5-10

### The Eruption Event - September 19, 1994

**Timeline (Historical Fact):**
- 6:20 AM: Mount Tavurvur erupts
- 8:00 AM: Mount Vulcan erupts (simultaneous)
- 9:00 AM: Ash column reaches 20,000+ feet
- 11:00 AM: Complete darkness (midday)
- Ongoing: Volcanic gases, tremors, pyroclastic flows
- Days following: Ash falls across entire region

**Impact (Documented):**
- 50,000 evacuated
- City rendered uninhabitable
- Infrastructure destroyed
- Power/water/communications down
- Rescue operations compromised
